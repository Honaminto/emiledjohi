2025 — Nassau International Wushu Championships (USA)

USAWKF National Kungfu Team Trials
Nassau County, New York

At the Nassau International Wushu Championships, held as part of the USAWKF National Kungfu Team Trials, I competed in four elite divisions, demonstrating versatility across modern and traditional Wushu disciplines.

Results: 3 Gold Medals · 1 Bronze Medal

  • Changquan (Long Fist) — Gold Medal
    High-difficulty modern routines emphasizing speed, balance, flexibility, and explosive power.
  • Jianshu (Straight Sword) — Gold Medal
    Precision-based sword forms combining agility, fluid transitions, and technical accuracy.
  • Shaolin Tanglang Quan (Praying Mantis Forms) — Gold Medal
    Traditional Shaolin-derived routines highlighting close-range combat techniques, rhythm, and tactical movement.
  • Qiangshu (Spear) — Bronze Medal
    Long-weapon performance requiring strength, coordination, and advanced spatial control.

17th Golden State International Wushu Championships

San Jose, California

At the 17th Golden State International Wushu Championships, one of the largest international Wushu events in the United States, I competed in four categories against a highly competitive international field.

Results: 2 Gold Medals · 1 Silver Medal · 1 Bronze Medal

  • Changquan (Long Fist) — Bronze Medal
    Competitive modern routines judged on athleticism, execution, and overall performance quality.
  • Jianshu (Straight Sword) — Gold Medal
    Awarded for technical precision, expressive movement, and superior weapon control.
  • Shaolin Tanglang Quan (Praying Mantis Forms) — Silver Medal
    Traditional forms emphasizing structure, timing, and authentic stylistic expression.
  • Qiangshu (Spear) — Gold Medal
    Long-weapon performance recognized for dynamic movement, power, and technical mastery.
